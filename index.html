The developers from Double Click Leads figured out not only how to make it work on both desktop and mobile but also completely for Free. Not only that, they developed very easy to use software which allows you to post those Native Facebook Leads forms automatically to unlimited fan pages.

Double Click Leads is stand alone app. Since Connect Leads is for moving leads from Facebook to autoresponder it is used for leads already collected. Double Click Leads is used to collect those leads which later Connect Leads moves to autoresponders.

So using Double Click Leads by itself you get full benefit of free Facebook lead form distribution to collect leads, which then you can download as list from Facebook. Double Click Leads can work either together with Connect Leads or as standalone application

With Double Click Leads, potential customers can sign up, and you’ll get accurate contact info to follow up with them. When potential customers see your lead form on Facebook, they can sign up for more info or request something from your business. By clicking your lead form, customers will see a form that’s already filled with info they’ve shared with Facebook – like their name, number, or email.
